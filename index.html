How to use List?

Begin with 2-digit codes (01-03) and progress to higher digits to locate your specific code. The data is organized into lists and sublists. Refresh the page to reset the list for quick access.

How to use Search?

Click the search bar, input keywords or IDs to filter the list; note that all related data will appear, and IDs may not be in search order (e.g., '01' may show '0101', '0201', '0301', with '0101' first due to its closer relevance to your search term).
